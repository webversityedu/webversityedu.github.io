1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

We believe that a great learning environment is built on trust. This policy explains our practices in plain English. Our goal is to use data to improve your experience, not to exploit it.

Webversity ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website, services, and content (the "Platform") and explains how we handle your personal information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the information you give us (like your name and email when you sign up), data from your use of the platform (like course progress), and technical data (like your browser type).

2.1 Information You Provide

This includes personal data you provide when you create an account, update your profile, purchase a course, or communicate with us, such as your name, email address, password, and payment information.

2.2 Information from Platform Use

We collect data about your interactions with the Platform, such as the courses you enroll in, your quiz scores, your progress, content you view, and your interactions with instructors and other students.

2.3 Technical Data

Like most websites, we automatically collect certain technical data, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and operating system. This helps us optimize the platform for your device.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to run the platform, personalize your experience, and communicate with you. For example, we use your progress to show you what to learn next and your email to send important account updates.

  • To Provide and Manage the Platform: Authenticating your access, processing payments, and showing your course progress.
  • To Improve Our Services: Analyzing usage data helps us understand what features are working and how to make the platform better.
  • To Communicate With You: Sending you administrative messages, updates, and information about your courses. You can opt-out of marketing communications.
  • For Security and Safety: To prevent fraud, abuse, and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. When We Share Data

We don't sell your personal data. We only share it when necessary to provide our services, such as with your instructors (so they know who is in their class) or with trusted partners who handle payments and website hosting.

We may share your data with:

  • Instructors: We share your name and profile information with instructors for the courses you enroll in.
  • Service Providers: We work with trusted third parties to perform services like payment processing, cloud hosting, and email delivery. These providers only have access to the information necessary to perform their functions.
  • For Legal Reasons: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Webversity, our users, or the public.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use standard physical, technical, and administrative security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

While no system is completely secure, we implement safeguards like data encryption and access controls designed to protect your information. It is also important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your password and your computer.

6. Your Rights & Choices

You have control over your data. You can access and update your account information at any time. Depending on your location, you may have additional rights, such as the right to request deletion of your data.

You can review and change your personal information by logging into your account profile. You can also opt-out of receiving promotional emails from us by using the "unsubscribe" link in those emails. For any requests regarding your personal data, please contact us.

7. Cookie Policy

We use cookies (small text files) to make our site work, for example, to keep you logged in. We also use them for analytics to help us improve the platform. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Cookies are essential for the operation of the Platform. By using our services, you agree to the use of these cookies. Our use of analytics cookies helps us understand user behavior to enhance the user experience.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help.

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@Webversity.com.
