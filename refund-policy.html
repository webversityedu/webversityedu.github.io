Our Philosophy on Refunds

Our goal is your satisfaction. This policy is designed to be fair, allowing students to feel secure in their purchases while protecting our educators from fraud after their valuable content has been consumed.

We believe in the quality of the courses on our platform. However, we understand that sometimes a course may not be what you expected. This policy outlines when you can request a refund.

Refunds for Self-Paced Courses

For pre-recorded courses, you are eligible for a refund within 14 days of purchase, provided you have not consumed a significant portion of the course content.

  • check
    14-Day Window: Refund requests must be made within 14 calendar days of your purchase date.
  • hourglass_empty
    Limited Consumption: You are eligible only if you have viewed less than 25% of the total course video content. This ensures fairness to our educators.
  • close
    No Bulk Refunds: We reserve the right to deny refunds to users who exhibit a pattern of buying and refunding Webple courses.

Refunds for Live Cohorts

For live cohorts with monthly payments, refunds are generally not provided for past months. For your first month, you may be eligible if you request a refund early in the term.

  • check
    First Month Refund Window: You may request a refund for your first month's payment within 7 days of the cohort start date, or before the 2nd live class, whichever comes first.
  • close
    Past Months: No refunds are issued for previous months of a cohort, as the service (live classes and access) was delivered during that time.
  • cancel_schedule_send
    Cancelling Future Payments: You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. This will prevent any future charges but will not refund past payments.

What's Not Eligible for a Refund

We must protect our platform and educators from fraud. Refunds will be denied in cases of abuse or after significant value has been received by the student.

  • close
    Significant Consumption: Requests for courses where more than 25% of the content has been viewed.
  • close
    Requests Outside the Window: Any request made more than 14 days after purchase (for courses) or after the first month's refund window (for cohorts).
  • close
    Account Suspension: Students who have been suspended or banned for violating our Terms of Service are not eligible for refunds.

How to Request a Refund

We've made the process simple. You can submit a request directly from your student dashboard, and our team will review it.

To request a refund, please navigate to your "Payments" tab in your student dashboard, find the purchase you wish to refund, and click the "Request Refund" option. Please provide a brief reason for your request to help us process it quickly. Our support team will review your request and respond within 5-7 business days.
