Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any other fees besides the platform fee? expand_more

Our platform fee is all-inclusive for our services. The only other fee is the standard processing fee charged by our payment gateway partners (like Stripe or Razorpay), which is typically around 2-3%. We do not add any extra charges on top of that.

How do I get paid? expand_more

Payouts are handled automatically. Once a student pays, your share of the revenue (70% for courses, 60% for cohorts) is held securely and paid out to your connected bank account on a monthly basis. You can track all expected payouts in your Payments dashboard.

Is there a minimum price I have to set? expand_more

Yes, to maintain the quality of the platform, we have a minimum price of ₹300 for one-time courses and ₹500/month for live cohorts. You have complete autonomy to set your price anywhere above these minimums based on the value you provide.
